HOW TO USE YOUR FSH EMPIRE DASHBOARD
🚀 GETTING STARTED
Step 1 — Create an account
Click Register. Fill in account details on Step 1, then contact details on Step 2. Confirm your email then sign in.
Step 2 — Connect Deriv (optional)
Click ⚡ DERIV button top right. Paste your API token from app.deriv.com/account/api-token. Balance and trades sync automatically.
Step 3 — Upload your trades
Go to All Trades tab. Choose CSV, HTML, screenshot, or paste. Select account/firm from dropdown then save.
Step 4 — Start journaling
Go to Journal tab after each trade. Log emotion, reasoning, what went well and what to improve.

❓ FAQ
Is my data safe?
Yes. Stored in your private Supabase database. Each user only sees their own data.
Will data be there on another device?
Yes. Everything is in the cloud. Log in from any phone, tablet or computer.
Can other traders register?
Yes. Each trader gets private data — they cannot see your trades and you cannot see theirs.
What is the FTMO risk meter?
Tracks drawdown and profit against prop firm rules. Bars turn red as you approach limits.
How do I delete a trade?
Go to supabase.com → your project → Table Editor → trades table. In-dashboard delete button coming soon.
