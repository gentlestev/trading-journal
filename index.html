HOW TO USE FSH EMPIRE
🚀 GETTING STARTED
Step 1 — Select your firm tab
Click Deriv, FTMO, The5ers, or Other from the top menu.
Step 2 — Upload trades
Use ⬆ Upload Trades sub-tab. Drop CSV/HTML, screenshots (AI), or paste text. Trades save directly to that firm.
Step 3 — Review stats
Overview shows equity curve, daily P&L, risk limits. Trade History shows every trade.
Step 4 — Journal & Analysis
Each firm has its own Journal and Analysis sub-tab for focused per-firm tracking.
⚡ DERIV API
1. Go to app.deriv.com/account/api-token
2. Switch to Real account (not Demo)
3. Create token — enable Read and Trading information
4. Click ⚡ DERIV button top-right and paste token
