HOW TO USE YOUR FSH EMPIRE DASHBOARD
🚀 GETTING STARTED
Step 1 — Select your firm tab
Click Deriv, FTMO, The5ers, or Other from the top menu.
Step 2 — Upload trades for that firm
Go to ⬆ Upload Trades sub-tab. Upload CSV, HTML, screenshots, or paste. Trades are saved under that firm automatically.
Step 3 — Review stats & history
Use Overview for stats, Trade History to see all trades for that firm.
Step 4 — Journal & Analysis
Each firm has its own Journal and Analysis sub-tab for focused tracking.
⚡ DERIV API
1. Go to app.deriv.com/account/api-token
2. Switch to Real account (not Demo)
3. Create token — enable Read and Trading information
4. Click ⚡ DERIV button top right and paste token
Token is saved on your device only.
